Welcome to the Mapbassadors Project!

Based out of Hoover, Alabama, this website is the result of a four month senior design project in our Engineering Academy. Our team is made up of Andy Kong, Skylar Thornton, Tony Sullivan, and Gerald. While our main functionality isn't up yet, we are halfway through our second Sprint to finish this website. While the front is done, the gears and behind-the-scenes stuff must be finished, whereupon Hoover will have a new resource for visitors that get lost in our 500,000 sqft school. Come to the school and scan our QR code to access the mapping functionality!
