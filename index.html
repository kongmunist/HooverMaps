Welcome to the Hoover Maps Project!

A senior design project of the Engineering Academy at Hoover High School, this website is a mapping algorithm guiding you through our 500,000 sqft school's nooks and crannies. Our team is below, and our functionality is above.

How do I use this thing?

Click on Input Location to tell us where you are and where you're trying to get to, and a slideshow of your path along with text instructions will be generated and displayed.

I have a suggestion to make...

Dislike our choice of landscape over portrait pictures? Willing to sue because our color scheme hurt your eyes? Love it and want us to smile when we read through all these suggestions? Hit the Feedback tab above and let us know what you think!
